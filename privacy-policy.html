Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

SEO Experts Denver, a resource powered by Eye To Ad Media, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit seoexpertsdenver.com, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

By using this website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use this website.

Information We Collect

We collect information in two ways: information you provide directly, and information collected automatically through standard web technologies.

Information you provide directly includes:

  • Name, phone number, email address, and ZIP code submitted through our contact or audit request form
  • Any notes or messages you include with a form submission

Information collected automatically may include:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Pages visited, time spent, and links clicked
  • Referring website or source

This automatically collected information is used in aggregate to understand how visitors use the site and to improve its content and performance. It is not used to personally identify individual visitors.

How We Use Your Information

Information you submit through our contact form is used solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry or audit request
  • Contact you about your specific request via phone or email
  • Provide the service or information you requested

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your contact information will never be added to marketing lists without your explicit consent.

Third-Party Services

This website uses the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy practices:

  • EmailJS — used to process and deliver form submissions. Form data is transmitted securely to our email account. EmailJS privacy policy: emailjs.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • Google Fonts — used to load web fonts. Google may collect limited data related to font requests. Google Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or any third-party behavioral advertising services on this website.

Cookies

This website does not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes. Any cookies that may be set are strictly functional — for example, session-related cookies set by third-party services like Google Fonts. These cookies do not collect personally identifiable information and are not used to track your activity across other websites.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information submitted through this website. Form submissions are transmitted via secure HTTPS connections and processed through EmailJS's encrypted infrastructure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained in our email system for the purpose of responding to your inquiry and maintaining a record of business communications. If you would like your information removed, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to any personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of any communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Children's Privacy

This website is intended for business owners and marketing professionals. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 13. If you believe a child has submitted information through our website, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of this website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
