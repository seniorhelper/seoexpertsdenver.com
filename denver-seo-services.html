Technical SEO

Technical SEO is the foundation that determines how well all other SEO work performs. A site with crawl errors, slow load times, poor mobile experience, or broken internal links cannot rank at its full potential — regardless of how strong its content is.

We conduct thorough technical audits that surface every issue affecting your site's search performance, then prioritize and implement fixes based on impact. This is often where the fastest ranking improvements come from for established Denver businesses.

  • Full technical audit using Google Search Console and specialized tools
  • Crawl error identification and resolution
  • Core Web Vitals and page speed optimization
  • Mobile-first indexing compliance
  • Canonical tag implementation and duplicate content resolution
  • XML sitemap and robots.txt optimization
  • Schema markup and structured data implementation

AI Search & Generative Engine Optimization

AI-powered tools — ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Perplexity, and Gemini — are reshaping how Denver customers discover businesses. What SEO services do you offer for Denver businesses?

How is your Denver SEO approach different?

We combine traditional SEO fundamentals with AI and GEO optimization — so Denver businesses are visible in both Google's standard results and in the AI-generated answers that ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity produce.
