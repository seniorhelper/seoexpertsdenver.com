Accessibility Statement

Last updated: March 2026

Our Commitment to Accessibility

SEO Experts Denver, powered by Eye To Ad Media, is committed to making this website accessible to all visitors — including those who use assistive technologies such as screen readers, keyboard-only navigation, or other adaptive tools.

We believe that everyone deserves equal access to information. Accessibility is not an afterthought for us — it is part of how we build. This website has been designed and developed with accessibility standards in mind from the ground up.

Standards We Follow

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA, published by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C). These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with disabilities.

Our implementation includes the following accessibility features:

  • Semantic HTML5 markup throughout all pages
  • Skip-to-content link at the top of every page for keyboard users
  • All interactive elements are keyboard accessible and have visible focus states
  • Sufficient color contrast ratios on all text and background combinations
  • Descriptive alt text on all informative images
  • ARIA labels and roles on navigation, forms, and interactive components
  • Form fields have associated visible labels
  • Pages are structured with logical heading hierarchies (H1 through H4)
  • All pages are fully responsive and usable on mobile devices
  • Language declared on every page with the lang attribute
  • Live regions used for dynamic content updates (form status messages)
  • No content relies solely on color to convey meaning
  • No flashing or strobing content that could trigger photosensitive responses

Known Limitations

While we strive for full WCAG 2.1 AA compliance, we acknowledge that some areas of this website may not yet fully meet all guidelines. We are actively working to identify and address any gaps. If you encounter an accessibility barrier, please let us know — your feedback helps us improve.

Assistive Technology Compatibility

This website is designed to be compatible with the following assistive technologies and browsers:

  • Screen readers including NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver (macOS and iOS), and TalkBack (Android)
  • Keyboard-only navigation in all modern browsers
  • Browser zoom up to 200% without loss of content or functionality
  • High contrast display modes on Windows and macOS
  • Text-only browsing environments

Feedback and Contact

If you experience any difficulty accessing content on this website, or if you would like to report an accessibility issue, please contact us. We take all accessibility feedback seriously and aim to respond within 5 business days.

  • Phone: 1-800-481-8638
  • Website: Contact form

When contacting us about an accessibility issue, please include the URL of the page you were visiting, a description of the problem you experienced, and the assistive technology or browser you were using. This information helps us diagnose and address the issue more effectively.

Ongoing Improvement

Accessibility is not a one-time project. We conduct periodic accessibility reviews and update our practices as guidelines evolve. We are committed to continuous improvement and welcome the input of users with disabilities in shaping a more accessible experience.

This statement was prepared in March 2026 and will be reviewed and updated at least annually.
